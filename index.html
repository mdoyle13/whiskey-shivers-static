Watch Whiskey Shivers as "Saddle Up" in Pitch Perfect 3

Check us out as "Saddle Up" in Pitch Perfect 3


“WHISKEY SHIVERS are actually friends of mine, and I truly just emailed them one day and said, ‘Can you guys please come be in Pitch Perfect?’ And they probably thought I was kidding or crazy or delusional or drunk, but they’re like,‘Sure.’ And so here they are. They’re barefoot most of the time. They drive around in a van. They play a suitcase for a kick drum. This is them. They are down- home, salt-of-the-earth guys.” - Trisch Sie

“The boys in WHISKEY SHIVERS are the most talented, sweet, funny guys. I feel very blessed that we get to work with them. When we have downtime, the instruments are always set up for a lot of our scenes, so I’d go and try to learn how to play drums. It’s very collaborative, and it’s fun to work with very gifted musicians who also just seem to be able to lend themselves to acting so naturally.” - Ruby Rose
